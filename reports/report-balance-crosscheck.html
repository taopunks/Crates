FINDING 1The 500-RAO constant — spendable vs raw free

Our lane (eth_getBalance) and theirs (System.Account.free via state_getStorage) disagreed by exactly 500 RAO on both accounts — the existential deposit. Frontier's EVM lane reports the reducible (spendable) balance; raw substrate free includes the deposit. Neither lane is wrong — they answer different questions. FINDING 2Their u64 caveat — proven, not just believed

The reference warns that Bittensor's System.Account balances are u64, not u128 as on Polkadot mainnet — mismatched width assumptions decode to silent garbage. We proved it from the blob itself: the returned AccountInfo is 56 bytes on both accounts (u64 layout); Polkadot's u128 layout would be 80. Decode offsets on chain 964: free @16 · reserved @24 · frozen @32, all u64 LE.

FINDING 3Stake lanes agree — including on emptiness

Their stake lane is a runtime-API sweep (StakeInfoRuntimeApi_get_stake_info_for_coldkey, a compact-SCALE vector of 9-field StakeInfo structs — truncate the struct and the decoder desyncs, exactly as they warn). Ours is the 0x805 precompile getStake(hotkey, blake2_256("evm:"‖H160), netuid) per known position. Run live on Agent #1's TBA — which registered SN116 UID 86 and later harvested its alpha back — both lanes returned empty/zero: chain-true, and in agreement.

METHOD NOTE — WHEN TO USE WHICH LANE

The sweep is the stronger read for total AUM: it finds every position under a coldkey, including ones you didn't know to ask about. Our precompile reads are position-scoped by construction — exact, cheap, but only as complete as the position list. Their SwapRuntimeApi_current_alpha_price (netuid as u16-LE) also verified live — SN116 priced at 0.040524601 τ/α at check time — a third, indexer-free lane for valuing alpha.

⚠ FORWARD FLAG — BANKED BEFORE IT BITES

Reserved balance is 0 on every account we touch today — but proxy deposits reserve TAO. The moment the 0x80b proxy precompile enters our stack (createPureProxy/addProxy both take deposits), a free-only display will silently understate holdings. The BM total is free + reserved + root stake + Σ(alpha × price); ours must grow the reserved term the same day proxies land.
